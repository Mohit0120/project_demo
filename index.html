Welcome to Neoskill

Neoskill is a UK based training
organisation. With over 20 years
experience in training, we specialise in
providing customised business to
business training solutions, from
requirements stage through to delivery.

At Neoskill we work on a variety of training projects, ranging
from soft skills to systems training, to improve workplace
effectiveness. We offer training UK-wide and internationally,
using a variety of delivery methods and innovative approaches
to achieve exceptional results. Unlike many training providers,
Neoskill have a team of permanent trainers to ensure flexibility
and training continuity for all our clients.
